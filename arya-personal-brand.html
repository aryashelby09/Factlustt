// About Me

The Story Behind the Screen

Where cricket passion meets digital creativity.

I'm Arya — a content creator who lives and breathes cricket while building digital experiences that connect communities. My journey started on the pitch and evolved into a mission to share knowledge, entertainment, and authentic stories with the world.

Through @factlustt on Instagram, I blend cricket insights, fun facts, and digital innovation into content that resonates with fans and tech enthusiasts alike. Every post is crafted with intention, passion, and a commitment to quality.
